* Origin country
* Destination country
* Shipper name and country
* Consignee name and country
* Declared means of envoyance should be by air
* AWB pieces and Phytosanitary Certificate pieces should match
* If weight is specified in Phytosanitary Certificate it should match with AWB weight
* If the Phytosanitary Certificate is digitally signed, it should be marked as digitally signed
* Nature of goods should be in line with the name of the product mentioned in the Phytosanitary Certificate. It may not match exactly
* Date of issue in the Phytosanitary Certificate should be earlier than the execution date in the AWB

## GUATEMALA
### Origin Requirements
- RUT/EIN/TIN
- ORIGINAL MAWB #2 (Digital)
- ORIGINAL HAWB #2 (Digital)
- CONSOLIDATED MANIFESTO (Digital) including all HAWBS

### AVI Requirements
- SHIPPER
- HEALTH DOCUMENTS
- CITES
- Certificate of Rabies Vaccine
- Sanitary permits (Original/Copy)

### PER Requirements
- ORIGINAL PHYTOSANITARY
- PRE-ALERTS

### HUM Requirements
- Death certificate
- Embalming Certificate (If applicable)
- Transfer permit from the country of origin
- PERMITS FROM THE AUTHORITIES OF ORIGIN AND DESTINATION

### DG Requirements
- NOTOC
- Original DGD, 2 copies

### International Transit/Transshipment
- RUT/EIN/TIN
- ORIGINAL MAWB #2
- ORIGINAL HAWB #2
- CONSOLIDATED MANIFESTO including all HAWBS

### International Domestic Transit
- RUT/EIN/TIN
- ORIGINAL MAWB #2
- ORIGINAL HAWB #2
- CONSOLIDATED MANIFESTO including all HAWBS

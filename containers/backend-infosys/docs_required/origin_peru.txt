## PE (Peru)
### Origin Requirements
- RUC/DNI/PASSPORT#
- ORIGINAL MAWB #2 (Digital)
- ORIGINAL HAWB #2 (Digital)
- CONSOLIDATED MANIFESTO (Digital) including all HAWBS
- WAREHOUSE (digital)

### AVI Requirements
- AVI'S STATEMENT
- HEALTH CERTIFICATE
- CITES
- Check the date of the Rabies Vaccine Certificate according to the country of destination

### PER Requirements
- Original phytosanitary product (Customer response) asparagus and Mango fruit

### HUM Requirements
- Death certificate
- Embalming Certificate (If applicable)
- Transfer permit from the country of origin

### DG Requirements
- Original DGD, 2 copies (digital)

### International Transit/Transshipment
- Accept digital copy Original 2 (digital)

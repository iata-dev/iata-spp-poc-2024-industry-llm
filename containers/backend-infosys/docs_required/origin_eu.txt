## EUROPEAN UNION
### Origin Requirements
- ACC3
- ORIGINAL MAWB #2 (Digital)
- CONSOLIDATED MANIFESTO (Digital) including all HAWBS

### AVI Requirements
- Certificate of Rabies Vaccine
- CITES

### PER Requirements
- ORIGINAL PHYTOSANITARY
- ORIGINAL MAWB

### HUM Requirements
- Death certificate
- Embalming Certificate (If applicable)
- Transfer permit from the country of origin
- PASSPORT OF THE HUM or Person accompanying/dispatching

### DG Requirements
- Original DGD, 2 copies

### International Transit/Transshipment
- ACC3
- INFORMATION IN THE FFM OF BOTH THE MAWB AND THE HAWB 4 HOURS BEFORE ARRIVAL
- PEP: Original Phytosanitary
- PES: Certificate of origin
- Lisbon (LIS), Portugal requirements:
  - General Cargo: copy of AWB, commercial invoice, packing list
  - Perishable: copy of AWB, commercial invoice and phytosanitary certificates

### International Domestic Transit
- ACC3
- MAWB (Copy)
- CONSOLIDATED MANIFESTO including all HAWBS
- Accept digital copy if originals are lost

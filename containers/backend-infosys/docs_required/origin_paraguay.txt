## PY (Paraguay)
### Origin Requirements
- RUC # Mandatory in cnne box
- ORIGINAL MAWB #2 (Digital)
- ORIGINAL HAWB #2 (Digital)
- CONSOLIDATED MANIFESTO (Digital) including all HAWBS

### AVI Requirements
- Certificate of Rabies Vaccine
- CITES

### PER Requirements
- Carnet, fitosanitarios original

### HUM Requirements
- Death certificate
- Embalming Certificate (If applicable)
- Transfer permit from the country of origin

### DG Requirements
- Original DGD, 2 copies

### International Domestic Transit
- N/A

## MX (Mexico)
### Origin Requirements
- ORIGINAL MAWB #2 (Digital)
- ORIGINAL HAWB #2 (Digital)
- CONSOLIDATED MANIFESTO (Digital) including all HAWBS

### AVI Requirements
- Certificate of Rabies Vaccine
- CITES

### PER Requirements
- ORIGINAL PHYTOSANITARY (Customer Responsibility)

### HUM Requirements
- Death certificate
- Embalming Certificate (If applicable)
- Transfer permit from the country of origin

### DG Requirements
- Original DGD
- 2 copies if the origin is MEX
- 1 Copy if it is TTO or MEX destination

### International Transit/Transshipment
- ORIGINAL MAWB #2
- ORIGINAL HAWB #2
- CONSOLIDATED MANIFESTO including all HAWBS

### International Domestic Transit
- COMMERCIAL INVOICE
- ORIGINAL MAWB #2 and 4
- ORIGINAL HAWB #2 and 4
- CONSOLIDATED MANIFESTO including all HAWBS
